Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by searching for your medicines and adding them to the cart, or by uploading a valid prescription.

What are the delivery times?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the availability of the medicines. You can see an estimated delivery time during the checkout process.

Is it safe to buy medicines online?

Yes, we ensure that all medicines are sourced from reputable suppliers and stored and handled according to safety standards. Your data is also kept secure.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets. Cash on delivery may be available in select locations.

How do I upload a prescription?

You can upload your prescription during the order process or by clicking the "Upload Prescription" button in the header or hero section. Please ensure the image is clear and readable.

Can I return or exchange medicines?

Our return and exchange policy depends on the type of medicine and the reason for return. Please refer to our detailed Return Policy linked in the footer for more information.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use this link to get real-time updates on your delivery status.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes, we offer online consultations with qualified pharmacists to help you with your medication queries.
